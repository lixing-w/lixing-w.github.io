Music

Here is a collection of my music. I love exploring electronic sounds and experimenting with harmonies heavily influenced by Japanese music and jazz. Take a listen to some of my works.

Antenna

A calm, warm track featuring a looping guitar phrase, smooth pads, vocal elements, sus chords, and slightly heightened tuning, depicting a tranquil summer night.

I carefully humanized MIDI for the guitars by adding palm mutes, very short notes, and rubato in solo sections. To create an illusory feeling, I applied automation to randomizing the tuning of the piano, and used arpeggiator and pan automation for synths. Background noises were made by modulating saw waves and randomizing the pitch, simulating radio noises. I also simulated the sound of railroad crossing bell by playing block chords on a piano. Written in July 2024.

STELLA LOVE

An intense, upbeat electronic track with heavy experimentation on arpeggiators and distortion, vocal chops.

I layered two arps and each of them took different roles. One is downsampled and creates a glitched feeling, and the other has extended reverb that renders the soundscape. For the vocal part, I used SynthV, an AI engine, to generate vocal phrases and then applied regular processings to create the chops. Written in July 2024.

Wandering Wings

An impressionism piano solo written in my senior year of high school as I was studying Ravel's approach to music. Written entirely in MIDI (YES this is ALL MIDI!), the piece depicts the imagery of birds through fast arpeggios. Written in August 2023.
